First Meeting of the Southern Logic Seminar, University of Bath

The inaugural meeting of the Southern Logic Seminar will be held on Thursday 23 February at the University of Bath.

Talks will take place in 5 West 2.4 on the main Claverton Down campus.

Preliminary Programme

Our speakers for the afternoon will include:

  • Torie Barrett (Bath)
  • Alex Kavvos (Bristol)
  • Leo Lobski (UCL)
  • Guy McCusker (Bath)
  • Bruno da Rocha Paiva (Birmingham)

Venue

The main campus of the University of Bath is located on a hilltop just outside the city centre. It is within walking distance from Bath Spa train station, and is also connected to the city via the U1 bus. Further details on how to get to the university can be found here.

Talks will take place in 5 West 2.4, which is located in the Department of Pharmacy and Pharmacology. Anyone arriving before lunchtime can find the local participants in the Department of Computer Science, on the fourth floor of 1 West. The campus map might be helpful if you get lost.

Travel bursaries

We have a small budget to cover travel costs for speakers and participants. Priority will be given to young researchers, particularly PhD students. We are also able to provide some support for participants with caring or parenting responsibilities. Anyone who requires support should contact Thomas Powell in advance of the meeting.
